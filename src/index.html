Switching to Campus card!

Dear KBTU students, we will soon be introducing Campus Cards into circulation and operation.

A campus card is a personalized plastic card that combines the following functions:
Pass to the territory of the university - entrance / exit from the building;
Pass to the territory of the House of undergraduates and students of KBTU;
Library card;
Accrual of scholarships (for grant recipients);
Means of payment for goods and services with free banking services (crediting and withdrawing funds).
